Raleigh/Durham Music Studio

Music lessons are a vital element of a child's development. The discipline of practicing will enhance a child's growth while teaching them to understand and apprciate music.

Becky Henson is a proficient and experienced teacher of both piano and voice. She teaches in the Raleigh / Durham area. Guahan Music Studio is now accepting students for beginning piano, elementary piano, and voice.

Piano Lessons

Piano lessons are offered to beginning and elementary pianists. Focused skills include rhythm, interval training, note names, technique, and music literature. Students will need to purchase method books from a local music store (or online). Contact Becky for rates and availability.

Voice Lessons

Practically everyone desires to sing better. Skills covered during lessons will include posture, breathing, phonation, pitch, dynamics, and other voice techniques. Students will need to purchase music and/or instruction books from a local music store (or online). Contact Becky for rates and availability.
